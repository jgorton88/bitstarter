Spend more time on the stuff that matters

We are excited to announce the development of WeRace.com, a web-based team workout timing and participant management system. WeRace will let group members check-in from their own or another member's device, participate in tracking finish times, and review historical results in real time.

Working out by yourself can be fun, but having a group to suffer with is better :)

It has been said that knowing your numbers is an important factor in attaining better athletic performance. With course information entered, WeRace will do the work for you, calculating pace times and recording weather data for the workout location. Time the workout for the day's participants cooperatively with other users, including multi-leg/multi-sport courses. And, when its all over, quickly verify that everyone made it back safely from longer routes.

Rally your friends and keep them informed

Using integrated social media, keep team members informed of upcoming workouts and schedule changes. Show those who volunteered to run activities the love by tracking their efforts and encourage others to thank them. Use the race calendar feature to let others know what you're competing in, and if you need to, share a ride to the event. There will even be a way to write a review so others can learn from your experience.

About WeRace.net

WeRace.net is an idea that I have been thinking over for some time, based on my participation in training groups in the Rochester, NY area. The development "team" solely consists of myself, John Gorton. This effort represents my participation in Startup Engineering, a course normally offered as part of the Stanford University Computer Science program, but now available as a MOOC in an online format for free!

We need your help

As a part of the coursework, I am offering this Kickstarter-style page to let you know about the WeRace.net product and to ask for your support. You can help to make this product a reality by spreading the news. Please use the sharing buttons near the top of this page and let your friends on Facebook and Twitter know about WeRace.net!
